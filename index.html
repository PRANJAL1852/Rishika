Life of a Beautiful Queen

About Rishika

Born on August 13, 2005, in Raxaul, Bihar, Rishika is a person of intelligence, grace, and humor. She values family, education, and ambition above all. Her elegance and beauty are complemented by a strong mind and a warm heart.

Education

Rishika completed her 10th from St. Marry School and her 12th from ASBS School. She is currently in the 4th semester of English Honours at Shyama Prasad Mukherjee College, Delhi University. She has a deep appreciation for literature and aspires to work in a government position.

Interests

Rishika enjoys reading novels, drawing, and dancing. She also loves spending quality time with close ones and discussing literature, life, and dreams.
